# Triggers

#### Long positions

* **`BTO`** - Buy to Open
* **`STC`** - Sell to Close

#### Short positions

* **`STO`** - Sell to Open
* **`BTC`** - Buy to Close

{% hint style="info" %}
**Triggers & tickers are not case sensitive.**&#x20;
{% endhint %}

## Trigger Aliases

These work just as triggers do and are used at start of input.
